E-commerce Platform Scalability Enhancement

Revamped a struggling e-commerce platform to handle high traffic, eliminating downtime during peak sales.

The Problem

The client's existing e-commerce platform was experiencing frequent crashes during peak sales periods (e.g., Black Friday), leading to an estimated **15% loss in potential revenue** due to abandoned carts and frustrated customers. Manual inventory tracking also caused stockouts and overstocking.

My Solution

After a thorough system audit, I identified bottlenecks in database queries and inefficient server configurations. I implemented a caching mechanism using **Redis**, optimized database indexes, and migrated the platform to a scalable **microservices architecture on AWS (EC2, RDS)**. For inventory, I developed a real-time tracking system integrating with their POS, triggering automated reorder alerts using **Node.js** and **PostgreSQL**.

Measurable Results

  • Platform uptime increased to **99.9%**, eliminating peak-time crashes.
  • Page load times reduced by **40%**, contributing to a **10% increase in conversion rates**.
  • Automated inventory system reduced stockouts by **80%** and streamlined order fulfillment by **30%**.
  • Overall operational costs decreased by **5%**.

Automated Customer Support Ticket Routing

Developed an intelligent system to automatically route customer support tickets, reducing response times.

The Problem

The client's customer support team was overwhelmed by a high volume of undifferentiated tickets, leading to **average response times of over 24 hours** and significant agent burnout. Tickets often landed with the wrong department, requiring manual re-routing.

My Solution

I designed and implemented a machine learning-powered **ticket classification system** using **Python (scikit-learn)** and natural language processing (NLP) to analyze ticket content. This system automatically routes tickets to the most appropriate department or agent, integrating with their existing CRM via a **RESTful API**.

Measurable Results

  • Average ticket response time reduced by **60% (from 24+ hours to less than 10 hours)**.
  • Agent workload re-distribution improved efficiency by **25%**.
  • Customer satisfaction scores (CSAT) increased by **15%**.

Client Testimonial

"The automated routing system built by [Your Name] has been a game-changer for our support team. Tickets are now handled much faster, and our agents can focus on solving problems instead of sorting them. An invaluable improvement!" - John Smith, Head of Customer Service at TechSolutions

Technologies: Python, Flask, scikit-learn, NLTK, PostgreSQL, RESTful API Design
